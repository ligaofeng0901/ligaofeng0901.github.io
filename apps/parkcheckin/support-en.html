FAQ

1) Location button doesn’t respond / cannot center to my location

  • Check that Location Services is enabled on your device.
  • Check that the app has location permission (ideally “While Using the App” or higher).
  • If you previously denied permission, you must re-enable it in iOS Settings.

2) Photo scan shows no new photos / photos don’t match any park

  • Ensure the app has Photo Library permission.
  • If your photos don’t contain location metadata, matching may not be possible.
  • Inaccurate GPS (e.g., indoors) may reduce matching accuracy.

3) How does incremental photo scanning work? Will it slow down the app?

  • Scanning runs incrementally and prioritizes photos taken after the last scan.
  • Work is processed in batches to reduce UI blocking where possible.

4) Automatic location check-in does not trigger

  • Make sure you enabled the feature and granted location permission.
  • Automatic check-in usually requires staying in a park area for some time; drive-by passes typically won’t trigger.
  • If location accuracy is poor, the app may avoid making a decision to reduce false positives.

5) Pro purchase / restore

  • Make sure you are using the same Apple ID used for the purchase.
  • Use the “Restore Purchases” option in the purchase page.
  • If it still fails, email support with relevant purchase context (do not share sensitive credentials).
