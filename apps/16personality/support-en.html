About this app

16 Personalities offers Quick, Standard, and Full-length preference quizzes on your device, with history, type write-ups, and sharing. Progress and results stay on your device. No sign-up is required.

Requirements: iOS 16.0 or later.

FAQ

  • Will I lose progress if I leave mid-quiz? No. Each version saves automatically; open the same version again to continue.
  • Is my data uploaded? Quiz answers and history are stored on device only. They are not uploaded to the developer’s servers. See our Privacy Policy.
  • How do I unlock deep interpretation? From a type detail screen, choose to watch a full rewarded video ad. Closing early or skipping means content stays locked.
  • Ads won’t load? Check network, date & time, and region settings. Some networks block ad SDKs. Try again later.
  • Is this a medical or diagnostic tool? No. Results are for entertainment and self-reflection only, not psychological, career, or medical advice.
