About TraceWorld

TraceWorld is a personal exploration map. As you move, the app reveals the map in areas you have visited and can show your recent path. Exploration progress and levels are saved on your device. No account is required.

Getting Started

  1. Open TraceWorld and allow location access when prompted.
  2. For continuous recording in the background, go to Settings → TraceWorld → Location → Always.
  3. On the map, press and hold the center button at the bottom for about 1 second to turn auto-recording on or off.
  4. Tap the gear icon (top right) for language, map style, fog opacity, recent track, and more.

Location Permissions

While Using the App

Needed to show your position, record explored areas, and draw your recent track.

Always Allow

Needed only when auto-recording is on, so exploration and your recent path continue when the app is in the background or the screen is locked. You can turn auto-recording off at any time.

FAQ

Why isn’t my map revealing as I walk?

  • Check that location permission is granted.
  • Turn on auto-recording (long-press the center button).
  • For background use, set Location to Always in iOS Settings.
  • Make sure you are outdoors or have a good GPS signal.

Why is there a gap in my recent track?

Gaps usually mean location was denied, auto-recording was off, or the app was force-quit. Enable Always location and keep auto-recording on while moving.

How do I change language?

Settings (gear) → Language → Follow System, English, or Simplified Chinese.

How do I hide the recent track?

Settings → turn off Show Recent Track. You can also change track color there.

How do I delete my data?

Remove TraceWorld from your iPhone. All exploration and track data stored by the app on that device will be deleted. There is no cloud backup from us.

Does the app work on iPad?

TraceWorld is designed for iPhone only.

System Requirements

  • Location Services enabled
  • Internet connection for map tiles (Apple Maps)
